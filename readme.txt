A WordPress plugin that allows you to post a survey/poll to your WordPress site.

== Description ==
Sophware Analytics is a customizable survey platform that allows businesses of any size to collect insights about their customers, employees, or any other aspect of their business. Sophware Analytics will allow you to create surveys, collect responses, and analyze your responses. A Sophware Analytics account is required to use the plugin.  Register for free at [Sophware Analytics](https://analytics.sophware.com/register).

**Features**

* Fully customizable and easy to use.
* Publish on your site or ours.
* Secure access to response data, no one sees but you.
* Interactive result summary.
* Allows you to customize the look and feel of the survey on site. Including color, font size, and more!
* Display with your survey with a widget or with the shortcode: [survey].
* 7 Different question types. Free form, rankings, ratings, multiple choice, and more!

== Installation ==
1. Upload `SophwareAnalytics.zip` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the \'Plugins\' menu in WordPress
3. Register and create your survey at [Sophware Analytics](https://analytics.sophware.com/register). Then add your Campaign ID to the Sophware tab.
4. Use the shortcode: [survey] or the Sophware Analytics widget to display your survey.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Where can I find my Campaign ID? =

You can find your Campaign ID on the Sophware Analytics site. Follow the link on the Sophware tab and login to [Sophware Analytics](https://analytics.sophware.com/). Select the campaign you want to display and click "publish". Follow the instructions on the WordPress tab.
